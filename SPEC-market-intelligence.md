## TAM (Total Addressable Market)


### Nigeria Fashion Industry

- **Market size:** ~N3.5 trillion (2025)
- **Tailor shops:** ~500,000 (estimated)
- **Formal wear customers:** ~10 million annually
- **Wedding market:** ~2.5 million weddings/year

### Target Segment

- **Urban middle class:** ~15 million households
- **Annual income:** N3M - N15M
- **Willing to pay for quality:** 60%
- **Using WhatsApp for business:** 80%+

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

1. **Cusofer** (Nigeria) — Client management, limited
2. **TailorApp** (India) — Not localized for Nigeria
3. **SmartTailor** (China) — Basic, no RLS

### Indirect Competitors


- WhatsApp (manual tracking)
- Excel spreadsheets
- Paper notebooks

## Pricing Research

### Willingness to Pay

- **Solo tailor:** N3,000 - N10,000/month
- **Boutique:** N10,000 - N50,000/month
- **Fashion house:** N50,000+/month

### Recommended Pricing

- **Starter:** Free (limited clients)
- **Pro:** N5,000/month
- **Business:** N15,000/month
- **Enterprise:** N50,000+/month

## Opportunity

- **Problem:** Manual tracking = lost clients, errors, missed deadlines
- **Solution:** All-in-one digital solution
- **Differentiation:** Nigerian garments, local payments, WhatsApp
